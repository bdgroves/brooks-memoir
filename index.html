Memoir Iron Door Irregulars Yosemite Tuolumne Eastern Sierra Mono Lake
Iron Door Irregulars · Yosemite · Summer 1982

Daff Dome

The summer he was twelve, a broken-down Datsun decided the rest of Brooks's childhood. Over Sonora Pass to Mono Lake with his rock-hound mother and his friend Zack, then the car dies in Tuolumne Meadows and strands them for a week beneath Daff Dome — a Smurf city built and destroyed, a ropeless climb kept secret, both halves of a divided family converging on the same mountain road, and a yellow wire that brought the car, and the boy, home to the foothills for good. The centerpiece of the series.

Iron Door Irregulars · Yosemite · Early Summer 1982

Frank's Keys

Early summer 1982, weeks before Daff Dome, when the Datsun could still do anything. A caravan of hippies takes the gated back roads home from El Portal — Frank from the Dam in the lead with the keys to every locked gate, a river crossing the baby-blue wagon fords on its own power, and a 3 a.m. homecoming to the Evergreen Lodge, where a twelve-year-old is turned loose with a fistful of quarters and a supply of sarsaparilla. The Frank story, and the car's finest hour.
